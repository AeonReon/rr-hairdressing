Welcome to R&R

Your Neighbourhood Salon in the Heart of Coleraine

R&R Hairdressing is run by husband-and-wife team Ryan & Rebecca, right on Abbey Street in Coleraine town centre. With 20 years of combined experience between them, they've built a salon where every client gets the time, attention, and skill they deserve.
